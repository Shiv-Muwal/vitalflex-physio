About Us

Healing with Science,
Compassion & Expertise

Dr. Anjali is a highly skilled and dedicated physiotherapist with over 10 years of clinical experience in helping patients regain mobility, strength, and confidence. With an exceptional patient recovery rate of 98%, she has successfully treated a wide range of musculoskeletal, neurological, and post-surgical conditions, including many complex and challenging cases.
Her journey in the field of physiotherapy has been built on passion, perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to patient care. Through years of hard work, continuous learning, and countless success stories, she has established herself as a trusted name in rehabilitation and wellness.
Driven by a vision to deliver personalized, evidence-based treatment, Dr. Anjali founded VitalFlex Physio—a brand synonymous with excellence, compassion, and transformative care. Today, VitalFlex Physio stands as a testament to her dedication, offering patients a path toward pain-free living, improved mobility, and a better quality of life.
